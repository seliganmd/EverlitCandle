Easy Wallet Guide

Understanding your Everlit Candle and how to manage it.

How Your Candle Is Stored

When you light an Everlit Candle, we create a secure digital wallet for you automatically. This is called a "custodial wallet" — we manage the technical details so you don't have to.

Your candle exists as an NFT (non-fungible token) on the Solana blockchain, which means:

  • It's permanently recorded and cannot be lost
  • You truly own it — it belongs to your email address
  • You can view it anytime by signing into your account

Your Options

Keep It Simple (Recommended)

Leave your candle in your Everlit Candle account. We'll take care of everything — no extra apps, no passwords to remember, no technical knowledge needed.

Best for: Most users who want a peaceful, simple experience.

Transfer to Your Own Wallet

Move your candle to a personal Solana wallet like Phantom. This gives you full direct control and lets you see your candle in other apps.

Best for: Users familiar with crypto who want full ownership.

Setting Up Phantom Wallet

If you choose to transfer your candle to your own wallet, here's how to get started:

  1. Download the Phantom app from phantom.app or your app store
  2. Create a new wallet and save your recovery phrase somewhere safe
  3. In Everlit Candle, go to "My Candles" and select "Transfer"
  4. Enter your Phantom wallet address
  5. Confirm the transfer

Important: Never share your recovery phrase with anyone. Everlit Candle will never ask for it.
