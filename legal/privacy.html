Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Everlit Candle ("Everlit," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Prayer, memory, hope, intention, or other text submitted by you
  • Wallet address
  • IP address
  • Account information
  • Payment-related information processed by third-party payment providers
  • Analytics and usage information

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate Everlit Candle
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Process purchases and transactions
  • Display candles according to user preferences
  • Moderate public Candle Wall content
  • Improve and secure the service
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

Payments

Payments are processed by third-party providers such as Crossmint and related payment processors.

Everlit Candle does not store complete payment card information.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use the website and improve the service.

Analytics providers may collect information such as browser type, device information, pages visited, and IP address.

Public and Private Candles

Users may choose whether a candle is displayed publicly on the Candle Wall.

Public candles may be visible to other users.

Private candles are not displayed publicly.

Account Deletion

You may request account deletion by contacting: support@everlitcandle.com

Upon deletion:

  • Account information may be removed from our systems where legally permitted
  • Public candle visibility may be removed
  • Blockchain records and NFTs already recorded on-chain cannot be deleted

Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Payment processors
  • Blockchain infrastructure providers
  • Analytics providers
  • Service providers assisting operation of the platform
  • Government authorities when required by law

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Everlit Candle is not intended for children under 18 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be sent to: support@everlitcandle.com

Everlit Candle is a digital space for prayer, remembrance, and reflection. Content shared through candles represents personal expressions and is not professional advice of any kind.
