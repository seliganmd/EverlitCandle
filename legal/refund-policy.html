Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

1. Overview

Everlit Candle offers unique digital prayer candles as Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) on the Solana blockchain. Due to the immutable nature of blockchain technology, all sales are final once the NFT minting process has begun.

2. No Refunds Policy

All purchases are final and non-refundable.

Once you complete your purchase:

  • Your NFT is immediately minted to the Solana blockchain
  • The transaction is permanently recorded and cannot be reversed
  • The prayer intention and metadata are permanently stored
  • The digital candle becomes your permanent digital asset

3. Why No Refunds?

Blockchain technology ensures transparency and permanence, but also means:

  • Immutable Records: Once minted, an NFT cannot be "unminted" or deleted from the blockchain
  • Irreversible Transactions: Blockchain transactions are permanent and cannot be reversed
  • Network Fees: Gas fees and minting costs are paid to the Solana network and cannot be recovered
  • Digital Asset Delivery: The NFT is delivered instantly upon payment completion

4. Before You Purchase

We encourage you to verify all details before completing your purchase:

  • Review your prayer intention for accuracy
  • Confirm your email address is correct
  • Understand that you are purchasing a permanent digital asset
  • Verify your payment information

5. Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues during purchase:

  • Contact us immediately at support@everlitcandle.com
  • Provide your transaction details and email address
  • We will investigate and assist with any technical problems

Note: Technical support does not constitute grounds for refunds, but we will make reasonable efforts to resolve any system errors.

6. Unauthorized Transactions

If you believe your payment method was used fraudulently:

  • Contact your payment provider immediately
  • Notify us at support@everlitcandle.com
  • We will cooperate with any investigation

7. Contact Us

For questions about this Refund Policy, contact:

Email: support@everlitcandle.com
