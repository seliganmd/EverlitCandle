FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Everlit Candle?

Everlit Candle is a digital candle platform where you can light a unique candle connected to a prayer, memory, hope, or intention.

Do I need crypto?

No. You can pay by credit card. We create a custodial wallet for your candle automatically.

What does "on-chain" mean?

It means your candle is recorded on the Solana blockchain as proof that it exists and belongs to you.

What if EverlitCandle disappears?

Your candle lives permanently on the Solana blockchain, not on our servers. EverlitCandle provides the viewing experience, but your NFT belongs to you forever — regardless of what happens to our platform.

Can I keep my prayer private?

Yes. You can choose whether your candle appears anonymously on the Candle Wall or stays private.

Can I transfer the candle to my own wallet?

Yes. You can later transfer it to any Solana-compatible wallet.

How much does it cost?

Each candle costs $7.

Does the candle expire?

No. Your Everlit Candle is designed to remain permanently on-chain.
